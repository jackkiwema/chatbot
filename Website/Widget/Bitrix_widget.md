###### Develop Widget
* To embed bitrix widget to your website, go to CRM -> More -> Add-ons -> Website Widget
* Create Widget, Enter the name of the widget
* Change the appearance of the Background color of your widget, and icon color
* Disable the CRM form
* Configure your welcome message and auto welcome message
* Remove Bitrix 24 signature
* Save your settings
